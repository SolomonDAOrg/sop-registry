Instruments published here may have been adopted through the DAO's governance
mechanisms and may be **binding on the DAO and persons exercising authority
under the Company Agreement** to the extent provided by the Company Agreement
and applicable law.

Adopted SOPs and other instruments ratified through an Operational Pack are
Tertiary Instruments of the Company Agreement, incorporated by reference under
Operating Agreement Section 2.2. They are subject to the order of precedence
established in the Operating Agreement and do not override Primary or

That governance effect does **not** turn these materials into professional
